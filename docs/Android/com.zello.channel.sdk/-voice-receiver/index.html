VoiceReceiver

interface VoiceReceiver

Implement VoiceReceiver to provide custom handling for incoming voice data.

prepare will be called on your voice receiver when the incoming stream has finished opening. Then receive will be called repeatedly as new audio comes in from the channels server. When the stream is closing, onStreamStopped will be called.
